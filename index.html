ABOUT US

OUR JOURNEY

Founded in 2021, Agro Life Enterprise (Pvt) Ltd has quickly emerged as a provincial leader in Sri Lanka's agro industry. Registered under PV 236305, we have consistently grown to rank among the top 2-3 companies in the Southern Province. As a trusted importer and supplier of agrochemicals and customized fertilizers, we remain committed to fostering agricultural growth and innovation.

OUR MISSION & COMMITMENT

From our inception, we have been dedicated to importing high-quality agro-based fertilizers and providing tailored solutions to meet the needs of farmers. Our experienced field assistance team ensures that we maintain the highest standards in product quality and service. This relentless pursuit of excellence has propelled us to our current position, and we continue to drive advancements in Sri Lanka's agro sector.

FUTURE VISION

Looking ahead, we have already signed agreements with international partners, particularly in India, to strengthen our global reach. In the near future, we aim to hold exclusive marketing authority for key agro products in Sri Lanka.
